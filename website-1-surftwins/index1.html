WHO WE ARE

We are Mouhssin and Yassin, twin brothers from Essaouira, who have been passionate about surfing since age 10. Our love for this sport was accidental, discovered during our days spent on Essaouira's beautiful beaches. From the first wave, surfing ignited a fire within us that grew stronger over time. Our connection with the ocean, nature, and the sport deepened as we matured. The dream of opening our surf school gradually became our ultimate goal. Starting from scratch, we humbly worked alongside other schools, gaining invaluable experience and knowledge. Surfing is not just a hobby for us; it's a way of life. We eat, breathe, and sleep surfing and are eager to share our passion with you. Surfing goes beyond being a mere sport; it's a gateway to meeting fascinating individuals worldwide and sharing extraordinary experiences.
With over 7 years of experience as certified surf instructors, we have developed personalized teaching methods tailored to your level, ensuring that your progression and goals are easily attainable. We have successfully coached numerous individuals, guiding them from beginner to intermediate levels and helping them gain confidence in the water. We take immense pride in being ranked among the top 10% of Tripadvisor attractions worldwide in 2020.
Join the Surftwins Essaouira for an unforgettable experience that combines the expertise of two professional surfers with the beauty of the Moroccan ocean and sun. Prepare to have twice the fun as we embark on an incredible journey together!
